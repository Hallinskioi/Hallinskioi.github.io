My Mods and Maps

Below is a list of games I have made multiplayer maps and mods for.


Dawn of War: Dark Crusade/Soulstorm

Release Date: 2018 - present

I first started designing maps for Dawn of War: Dark Crusade back in early 2008. They were my first taste of the modding scene and in a way the mechanics behind the screen which would set me on my path for web development. I began making maps again in 2016 and since then I have released 13 files, 11 maps and 2 compilations generating over 13000 downloads.
These maps are made in a program called 'Mission Editor' which is part of the Dawn of War Mod Tools Collection.
The minimaps required for the multiplayer maps were made in GIMP. As the knowledge is becoming lost to time, I made a tutorial found here.
